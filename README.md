# 🚀 SkillLens.AI – Intelligent Resume Analyzer

**SkillLens.AI** is an advanced AI-powered web application designed to analyze resumes and provide actionable insights to enhance their quality. It helps users optimize their resumes for modern hiring systems by evaluating skills, keywords, and overall structure.

---

## 🌟 Overview

In today’s competitive job market, having a well-optimized resume is crucial. **SkillLens.AI** simplifies this process by delivering intelligent feedback, identifying skill gaps, and aligning resumes with industry expectations and job roles.

---

## ✨ Key Features

📄 **Resume Upload**
Upload resumes in **PDF or DOCX format** for instant analysis

📊 **Comprehensive Resume Scoring**
Get a detailed score out of **100** based on:

* Skills relevance
* Keyword optimization
* Experience & projects
* Resume completeness

🧠 **AI-Based Content Analysis**
Automatically extracts and analyzes:

* Skills
* Education
* Projects
* Work Experience

🎯 **Job Role Matching**
Evaluate your resume against roles like:

* Software Developer
* Web Developer
* Data Scientist

📉 **Skill Gap Identification**
Detect missing or weak skills required for your target role

📈 **ATS Compatibility Check**
Analyze how well your resume performs with Applicant Tracking Systems

💡 **Smart Recommendations**
Receive personalized suggestions to:

* Improve resume structure
* Add impactful keywords
* Enhance project descriptions
* Include measurable achievements
